# About Me

Hi there! I am a joint Ph.D. student of Harbin Institute of Technology (opens new window) and Microsoft Research Asia (opens new window), supervised by Prof. Xiangzhan Yu and Dr. Ming Zhou. I obtained my B.S. degree at Harbin Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Wanxiang Che (opens new window). Currently, I am working as a research intern at Natural Language Computing Group in MSRA, mentored by Dr. Yu Wu (opens new window) and Dr. Shujie Liu (opens new window). My research interests include self-supervised learning, speech processing and spoken language processing.
